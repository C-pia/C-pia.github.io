《C-pia! Magazine》


C-pia! is a free magazine that emulates the style and appeal of early-'00s bishoujo game magazines.

C-pia!'s main focus is on covering Adventure games and Simulation games released pre-2010, but we also look at old anime, classic RPGs, retro-focused creators, and modern indie titles that seek to recapture the charm of older games.
